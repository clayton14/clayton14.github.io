Majapahit empire 13-14th centuries

1.The most recent and most succesful empire of Indonesia and maybe all of Southeast Asia was the Majaphit empire.

2.The Majapahit empire was the last Indianized kingdom in Indonesia.

3. It was in eastern Java and it lasted in between the 13th and 14th centuries

4. In 1292 Mongol soldiers came to Java to arrange an insult to be made to Kublai Khan as an excuse to invade.

5. The empire was very powerful and had realtions with China,Siam,and Champa.

Important cultural signs

1. In Jakarta, there is a vast park known as 'Taman Mini Indonesia' which means 'Small Garden of Indonesia'. Here you can see full size replicas of houses built by the many cultural groupsfound throughout the islands.

2. Most traditional houses in Indonesia use wood as the basic material. the house may be on stilts and beneath the floor may be used as shelter for domestic animals.

3. In some houses you may find objects which are believed to have magical powers.
